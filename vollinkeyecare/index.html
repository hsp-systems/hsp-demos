What we offer

Complete eye care for the whole family.

01
Comprehensive Eye Exams
Full vision and eye health evaluation for adults and children. Glaucoma testing, dilation, and prescription updates included.
02
Contact Lens Fittings
Expert fittings including hard-to-fit and bifocal contacts. Dr. Vollink is regarded as a specialist in complex contact lens cases.
03
Eyeglass Frames
Curated selection of frames for every style and budget. Our staff will help you find a pair that fits your face and your life.
04
Emergency Eye Care
Same-day appointments available for eye injuries, infections, and sudden vision changes. Call us first.
05
Pre & Post-Op Care
Full co-management for LASIK and other surgical procedures. We coordinate with your surgeon through every step.
06
Sunglasses & Sport Lenses
Prescription sunglasses and sport-specific lenses for an active West Michigan lifestyle. UV protection done right.
About Dr. Vollink

20 years. Two locations. One standard of care.
